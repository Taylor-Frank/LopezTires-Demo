About Us

CICERO'S
TIRE SHOP.

Lopez Tires & Wheels has been serving the Cicero community with fast, honest, and affordable tire service. We're a family-owned small business that treats every customer right — whether you need a quick flat fix or a full set of new rims.

Our indoor shop is clean, organized, and equipped with professional-grade alignment technology. English and Spanish spoken. We accept credit cards, debit, and NFC mobile payments.
